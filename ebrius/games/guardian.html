Guardian

  1. Genre: Side-scrolling Platformer, linear story
  2. Platform: Windows XP, Vista, 7 and 8
  3. Year: 2014

You play the role of a teddy bear tasked with the duty to protect your child owner. When a mysterious force kidnaps the other toy guardians, it is up to you to rescue them and find the culprit. Make your way through exotic worlds and nightmare-like landscapes to save your comrades!
