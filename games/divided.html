Divided

Genre: 3D Platformer, linear story

Platform: Windows XP, Vista, 7 and 8

Year: 2015

A civilization in the clouds is facing apocalypse; a crystal that binds the world together has been cracked and stolen, and reality as they know it is shattering. They develop the robot model 63214 to collect the crystal fragments, which have been scattered across dangerous, shattered landscapes. Only the AI has the intelligence and skill to determine the cause and save their world.
