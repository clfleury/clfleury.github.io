Altered

Genre: 3D Action/Adventure, linear story

Platform: Windows XP, Vista, 7 and 8

Year: 2016

You are a messenger tasked with delivering a letter to the capital of your country. In order to reach your destination, you need to pass through Norwood Forest, revered in tales as the home of evil shadows and spirits. With naught but a single lantern, it is up to you to safely make it through.
