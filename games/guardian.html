Guardian

Genre: Side-scrolling Platformer, linear story

Platform: Windows XP, Vista, 7 and 8

Year: 2014

You play the role of a teddy bear tasked with the duty to protect your child owner. When a mysterious force kidnaps the other toy guardians, it is up to you to rescue them and find the culprit.
