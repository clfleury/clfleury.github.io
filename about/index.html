About Ebrius

Ebrius was founded as a one-man game studio by Ryan Fleury, who has been programming and designing games since 2008. An avid C++ user, Ryan codes all of his projects using the Allegro and OpenGL libraries. He is also designs the puzzles, visual aesthetic, audio, story, and flow of each game. He has years of experience in pixel art style creation, and has more recently moved onto 3D model creation and texturing. While the primary designer and developer of each project, Ryan collaborates with his fellow classmate Pierce Hopkins on model design.

Ryan's development process consists of the following stages:

  • Premise Brainstorming
  • Story and Puzzle Storyboarding
  • Initial Engine Coding
  • Preliminary Art Design
  • Engine-based Level Design
  • Code-based Cutscene development
  • Music Production
  • Sound Production
  • Final Art Design
